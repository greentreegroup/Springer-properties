Apartment Rentals

Overview

Springer Capital Properties has an extensive portfolio of properties throughout Manhattan, Brooklyn and the Bronx. Rental units are available in a range of buildings, including pre-war, luxury, new construction developments, affordable multi-family developments, and condominiums and cooperatives. Apartments are available at many different price levels.


With a rich history in property management, we focus on providing quality, responsive service to tenants.


For a list of available apartments, please contact our Leasing Department at
(718) 884-7676.
