Lemle & Wolff Property Management

Lemle & Wolff Property Management is an industry leader, managing apartments in neighborhoods throughout New York City since 1938. Our goal is to provide excellent management services as we respond to our clients’ needs to keep buildings physically and financially healthy. Lemle & Wolff has a long history of working with affordable housing programs. The firm’s staff is continuously trained in the changes affecting this industry and the real estate management practice in general. Staff also bring years of experience working with tenant populations across different communities. Lemle & Wolff takes a comprehensive approach to its business emphasizing strong tenant relations, cost effective maintenance and proper financial controls.
